Hinterview × case study

Allowing thousands of recruiters to make data-driven decisions

A 0–1 insights dashboard consolidating key metrics into a single, user-friendly interface, for recruiters optimising their video outreach and managers setting team targets.

ROLESole UX/UI Designer
SERVICEProduct design
USERSThousands of recruiters
and their managers
TYPE0–1 product
Background

Hinterview Insights was developed to give recruiters a comprehensive view of their activities, enabling data-driven decisions and improving workflow efficiency around video outreach. The feature consolidates key metrics and insights into a single, user-friendly interface, empowering recruiters to optimise their performance while allowing managers to set targets for their teams.

Challenges
  • Presenting complex data in an accessible, meaningful way
  • Easy to use for recruiters of all technical skill levels
  • 0–1 with no existing framework: the initial design and implementation were critical
Business goals
  • Promote data-driven decision-making in the video outreach process
  • Give recruiters insight into their video habits, showing that video is an effective tool and driving repeat use
Design

The final UI is a comprehensive, intuitive dashboard for accessing key metrics and insights. Data is presented clearly and meaningfully, letting recruiters make informed decisions quickly. Key actions are prominently displayed, and the user flow is optimised for ease of use.

Learnings
  • Understanding user needs and behaviours was crucial in creating a dashboard that's both efficient and engaging, even without formal user testing
  • An iterative design process allowed continuous refinement, ensuring the dashboard met both user and business needs

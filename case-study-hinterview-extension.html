Hinterview × case study

Reduced user friction with a streamlined recording process

A Chrome extension that lets recruiters capture and share candidate video interviews directly from their browser. No tab-juggling.

Background

The Hinterview Chrome Extension project aimed to enhance the recruitment process with an integrated tool for recruiters, streamlining the workflow so they can seamlessly capture and share candidate video interviews directly from the browser. The goal: improve user experience, increase productivity, and deliver a more efficient hiring process.

Challenges
  • An intuitive interface for capturing and sharing video interviews
  • Seamless integration with the existing recruitment platform
  • Easy for recruiters of all technical skill levels
Business goals
  • Increase the use of video interviews in the recruitment process
  • Improve overall workflow efficiency
  • Enhance recruiter satisfaction to drive repeat use
The approach

01Usability review

Designing 'Record a Hintro' from the ground up meant getting the fundamentals right: video capture and sharing integrated directly into the browser (avoiding the inefficiency of multiple tabs), a clear and accessible extension that recruiters could navigate with ease, and strategic placement of key actions to minimise clicks.

02User flows

Mapped the existing experience, then redesigned the flow around the idea that best fit business and user goals.

03Design

The final UI features a prominent, intuitive interface for capturing and sharing video interviews, an efficient workflow, and positive feedback that reinforces engagement. Consistent visual design and tone of voice align with Hinterview's branding.

Validated with users

The final UI was user tested; feedback showed a significant improvement in satisfaction and productivity, with recruiters saving an average of 30 seconds per video recorded.

Next steps
  • Monitor user interaction with the new feature
  • Gather feedback for continuous improvement
  • Explore additional features to further enhance the recruitment process
